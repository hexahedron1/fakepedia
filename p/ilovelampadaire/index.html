Ilovelampadaire
From Fakepedia, the made up encyclopedia
Not to be confused with Lucerna
The Ilovelampadaire (Lucerna viae), also known commonly as the lampyt, is a specimen of the genus Lucerna.

Etymology
The name "Ilovelampadaire" comes from a jumble of French (as it is this specimen's native language) and English. "Lampadaire" translates to "street lamp", so "Ilovelampadaire" essentially means "I love street lamps".

Biography
Ilovelampadaire was born in 2014[citation needed]. Ilovelampadaire is most active on the online chat platform Discord, on which he has a sizeable presence.

Integration into slang
In Flock slang, the verb "lamp" has been derived from Ilovelampadaire's name and has been given the meaning "to copy something, especially someone's idea, shamelessly".
