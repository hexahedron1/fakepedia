Nahui
From Fakepedia, the made up encyclopedia

Nahui is a quaint, beautiful village in the mountains of Peru. Nahui is located at -14.408, -71.3 in the Andes mountain range.

The location of Nahui on a map


Popularity in Slavic culture
In Slavic culture, Nahui is very well known and regarded as a symbol of wealth and prosperity. Often, people are told to go to the village. This is a gesture of respect and appreciation, as well as a wish of good luck.

Exports and production
Nahui is the largest producer of pure high-quality cocaine and is the reason why Peruvian cocaine is so well-known around the world. The village of Nahui produces and exports around 250 000 tons of cocaine per year. People speculate that this may be why the GDP per capita of Nahui is 200 000 USD, however others say it may be due to a free money glitch.
