dtpls
From Fakepedia, the made up encyclopedia

dtpls (species homo didesicus, dibbledop, (s)quiggletop, pibbleShop) is a species of humanoid machines originating in the chinese ethnostate of Romania.

Fun Fact!
A female dtpls can have up to 71 children in a single "gaming session". A baby dtps is called a dibble (or dibblet, depends on region[citation needed]).

Activities
The favorite game of a domesticated dtpls is Geometry Dash, though a wild dtpls prefers to play highqualitygame instead.

Vocalization
Every single dtpls has a neurological tendency to appreciate the word "hooah". This word has no meaning.
