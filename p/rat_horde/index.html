Rat Horde
From Fakepedia, the made up encyclopedia

A rat horde is a term used to refer to a cyberattack involving multiple computers being infected with remote access trojans.
This type of attack is characterized by simultaneous decreases in performance of desktop computers, possible unauthorized effects on the system, retrieval of personal data and the attackers being relocated to Nahui, Peru after they are caught.

Notable examples
* Attack on the Aciremans.
